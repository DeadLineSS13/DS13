10 January 2021

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed being able to load pulse rifle magazines into Heavy Pulse Rifle.

Nanako updated:

  • Significantly rebalanced the Contact Beam. Main firemode now has higher damage and armor pentration, but lower accuracy and longer charging time
  • Contact beam altfire now deals 50 burn damage to mobs around the user. Affects own tile as well, but not the user themselves
  • The contact beam now has a visual effect for charging up
  • Added new audio FX for contact beam.
  • Brute armor is now affected by the armor penetration of bullets and weapons. Seeker and contact beam both have very high AP
  • Fixed aiming mode on the contact beam being nonfunctional, and it now requires aiming to fire
  • Moderately reduced tripod's evasion, making it more vulnerable to heavy weapons.
  • Slightly reduced brute evasion.
  • Significantly increased cargo costs for contact beam.
  • Slightly reduced the armor penetration of plasma saw and laserpick.
  • Increased the biomass cost of T3 Necromorphs from 350 to 400
  • Reduced hunter's max health from 250 to 225.
  • When health hits 0, hunter is now downed for 30 seconds, previously 20.
  • Meddle signal ability no longer affects airlocks, doors, or shutters.
  • Tier II and III necromorphs now have minimum requirements of total biomass, meaning they can't be spawned at the beginning of the outbreak.
  • Fixed various issues with the hydrazine torch, it should work much better now
  • Fixed high velocity magazines not working in pulse rifles
  • Fixed ripper sawblades being invisible
  • Slightly increased the damage of pulse rounds. This affects pulse rifles, heavy pulse rifles, and pulse turrets

Terror4000rus updated:

  • DOM has proper channels in headset now.
  • DOM's cabinet now has DOM's access restriction.
  • Mining Foreman recieves vintage RIG from DOM's cabinet in closet.
  • Mining Foreman now has unique access for the closet.
  • If no message given in the signal's whispering, it cancels and refunds.
  • Signals now have three toggleable levels of night vision.
  • Guns with automatic firing shouldn't shoot after examing, pulling or checking a tile's content via hotkeys.
  • All vents now cycle air a lot faster, especially one in colony's airlocks.

02 January 2021

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed Spawn Menu bugging out and refusing to open.
  • In case of destruction of current spawn point, Marker will be set as one and a warning will be shown in necrochat.
  • Fixed being able to unsecure buildable turrets.

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Meddle spell, a way for signals to interact with the environment in a variety of minor mischievous ways. Try it out on everything you see!
  • Added the RIG retraction device, which allows powering down and retracting someone else's rig, as long as they don't object. Intended for medical use, and found in medical, surgery and emergency closets
  • Powering down a RIG now takes half as much time as sealing
  • Added the Riot RIG, a tough and heavy suit with extra melee protection.
  • RIG chest and boots can no longer be removed while the RIG is active
  • Reworked and standardised suit storage slots, to allow a much wider variety of things, including pouches, guns, tools, tanks, et al.
  • Fixed kinesis tether not visually attaching to the target
  • Fixed punishment not working correctly when using kinesis to throw a marker shard into disposals.

REsident55 updated:

  • External doors (the red ones!) as well as high security and vault doors have new Dead Space heavy door sounds.
  • Lightswitches and buttons have new Dead Space beep sounds.
  • All vending machines have new Dead Space browsing and purchase sounds.
  • A variety of Dead Space sounds for biotanks, and one for recycler interactions too.
  • Fixed bad punctuation in recycling tank interactions.
  • Removed some unused duplicate .oggs from the files, every little helps.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Fixed a present bug where pulse rifles would not accept HV magazines.
  • Fixed an issue in piping and power which caused issues in medical isolation.
  • Fixed an issue in the CSCIO's office where two consoles weren't able to be interacted with.
  • Increased SMO's office to fix a similar issue as above.
  • Fixed the missing taser from the CSECO's locker.
  • Fixed an accidental duplication of RIG spawns in the CSECO locker.
  • Removed the flash from base locker spawns. Will be re-added when they are fixed.
  • Fixed the door names of the Forensics Lab and Cryogenic Isolation in the Brig.
  • Minor adjustments to SMO and CSCIO's office to make them feel a little more fancy.
  • Moderately increased the Heavy Pulse Rifle's accuracy, minorly increased the damage factor, heat capacity and overheat cooldown.

06 December 2020

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed being unable to restock SCAF Bullpup and SWS Motorized Pulse Rifle magazines with appropriate ammunition.
  • Removed see_in_dark value changes that resulted in necromorphs not having dark vision.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • An experimental bugfix to departmental pagers not working.

30 November 2020

TheLion1675 updated:

  • !START OF EXPERIMENTAL BALANCING AND BUGFIXES!
  • The Kellion, Valor and Unitologist ERT types now all have a distinct access datum, locking their ship(s) to just them, including any of their controls. Unless you have an ID Card or have been granted access, you won't be able to hijack, enter or loot it.
  • Kellion team members now spawn with an ERT headset.
  • Kellion team members now spawn with individual rigs.
  • Kellion team members now get HUDs relevant to their position.
  • Increased Kellion's medical capability, which was previously a bit... Underwhelming.
  • Kellion's lockers are now used to store weapons prior to spawning, with one random spawn per round.
  • SecTec added to Kellion.
  • NanoMed Plus added to Kellion.
  • !END OF EXPERIMENTAL BALANCING AND BUGFIXES!
  • Temporarily removed flashbangs, teargas grenades and flashes from the Security vendors. They will be re-added when the intended fixes have been rolled out for Necromorphs getting stunlocked.
  • Removed a few R&D designs that made no sense or weren't for us.
  • Removed the 'Special Ops' crate that could be ordered by Cargo.
  • Laser Pickaxe added to R&D designs. Pick in dem heads! But... Really don't. I'm also a moderator, people. Please don't.
  • Significantly reduced the explosion radius and power of fueltanks. They're not portable mininukes anymore.
  • Reduced folded rollerbeds in size so they can now fit in storage.
  • Changed the item requirements to make a Divet from Silver to Plasteel.
  • Considerably lowered the requirements to make the Price Scanner in R&D.
  • Significantly lowered the requirements of some RIG accessories in R&D. Some of these may not be useful as of yet, but might soon be.
  • Contact Beam Energy significantly increased in cost.
  • Made the Contact Beam moderately more expensive to make.
  • Moderately decreased damage output from advanced circular saw.
  • Made the Contact Beam gun a huge item, whereas previously it was normal.
  • Replaced round end statistics to below the round type victory.
  • Renamed the laser pickaxe to rock saw, according to wiki lore.
  • Pulse High Velocity rounds can now be made by R&D. Their cost and price currently remain something that can be discussed.
  • Experimental fix to the wall of death screen one gets after being knocked unconscious at times.
  • Fixed an issue where the inner cargo warehouse shutter button wouldn't work.
  • Fixed the Colony's railings not being secured at round start.
  • Granted Security, Medical and Research access to the colony.
  • Added a bloodbank to the Aegis Colony Medical outpost.
  • Rejoice for our Science Mains! A fully operational and waiting facility has been added to the Aegis Colony for all your (Xeno)-Archeology desires! Idk, feeling cute, might discover an anomaly later kind of stuff!

Unknown updated:

  • Rebalanced the power of all material melee weapons. Especially the fire axe
  • Certain limbs can no longer be regrown with healing spells. This applies to divider limbs/head, and exploder pustule

20 November 2020

Jeser updated:

  • Increased rules window size.
  • Fixed rules window not loading rules.

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Advanced RIG, a rare, lightweight and tough suit, which is afforded to the chief engineer by default. You might be lucky enough to find one elsewhere
  • Added the Advanced Vital Monitor, a rig module that comes with a built in death alarm to notify emergency services of your untimely demise.
  • Advanced and hacker rig now both have insulated gloves
  • Slightly increased the range of puker's vomit, and reduced its windup time, both by 10%
  • Necromorph acid now metabolises a little faster, thus dealing damage faster. The total damage is unaffected
  • Pulse turrets now only require one power node to create. Formerly two
  • Slightly reduced the max range of the plasma cutter, it may not reach the screen edge when firing diagonally now.
  • Added repair kits, which can be used to perfectly repair other tools, or to repair any kind of object. They can be crafted from easily available materials and items.
  • Added the Tool Expansion recipe, which increases the maximum modification slots on a tool by one, at the cost of a power node.

  • An extra disposal pipe in the Engine SMES room has been removed.

  • The lockdown buttons in the Captain's Workstation now are set to the correct values.
  • Added a variety of sprites for various condiments.
  • Screll Snacks have been removed from vendors.
  • Various non-lore friendly dishes have been removed.
  • Various ingredients in freezer lockers have had the number in them reduced due to the introduction of Hydroponics.
  • Changed the sprite of Cuban Carp.
  • Fixed spelling errors in the descriptions of the shark food dishes.
  • Fixed the large Monkfish sprite being incorrect.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Changed the Lurker's mob size status to MOB_SMALL as opposed to MOB_MEDIUM.
  • Altered locker code. It will not fit one (1) medium sized mob.

  • Pulse rounds will no longer embed in victims
